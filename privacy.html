The Final Bet - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

Aitorzilla ("we," "us," or "our") operates The Final Bet Twitch Extension ("Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Twitch User ID: Your unique Twitch identifier (provided by Twitch)
  • Prediction Data: Your prediction choices, amounts wagered, and outcomes
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the Extension (clicks, time spent, features used)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, timestamps

Information You Provide:

  • Extension Preferences: Settings and configurations you choose
  • Support Communications: Messages you send to our customer support

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Financial information beyond Bits transactions through Twitch
  • Browsing activity outside the Extension
  • Personal messages or chat content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate the Extension: Process predictions, calculate payouts, maintain leaderboards
  • Improve User Experience: Personalize features and optimize performance
  • Provide Support: Respond to your questions and resolve issues
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve the Extension
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

With Twitch:

  • As required by Twitch's Developer Agreement
  • For Bits transactions and user authentication

Service Providers:

  • Trusted third parties who help operate the Extension (hosting, analytics)
  • These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption
  • Retention: We keep prediction data for as long as your account is active, plus up to 12 months for analytics
  • Security: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • Breach Notification: We will notify users of any data breaches as required by law

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out: Disable the Extension to stop data collection
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at aitorzilla@gmail.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your Extension preferences
  • Improve Extension performance
  • Analyze usage patterns

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect Extension functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

9. Third-Party Links

The Extension may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.
